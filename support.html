Magic Bullet - Support

Welcome to Magic Bullet support! Here you'll find answers to common questions and ways to get help.

What is Magic Bullet?

Magic Bullet is a mobile application that allows you to control and monitor compatible devices on your local network. The app connects to devices via WebSocket communication for real-time interaction.

Getting Started

Initial Setup

  1. Download and install Magic Bullet from the App Store
  2. Ensure your device and mobile phone are on the same Wi-Fi network
  3. Open the app and navigate to the connection settings
  4. Enter your device's IP address when prompted
  5. Tap Connect to establish the connection

Finding Your Device's IP Address

  • Check your device's network settings or configuration page
  • Look for "Network Information" or "Wi-Fi Settings"
  • The IP address typically looks like: 192.168.1.100 or 10.0.0.50

Frequently Asked Questions

Connection Issues

Q: The app says "Connection failed" or shows a timeout error

  • Verify both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network
  • Double-check the IP address you entered
  • Ensure your device is powered on and network-enabled
  • Try restarting both your mobile device and the target device

Q: The connection keeps dropping

  • Check your Wi-Fi signal strength
  • Ensure your router isn't blocking local network communication
  • Try moving closer to your Wi-Fi router

Q: I can't find my device's IP address

  • Check your router's admin panel for connected devices
  • Look for device documentation or settings menu
  • Try using network scanning apps to discover local devices

App Features

Q: How do I reset my connection settings?

  • Go to app settings and tap "Reset" or "Clear Data"
  • You can also uninstall and reinstall the app

Q: The app interface looks different than expected

  • Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  • Some features may vary based on your device capabilities

Technical Issues

Q: The app crashes or freezes

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your mobile device
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space
  • Update to the latest app version

Q: I'm seeing error messages

  • Most errors are related to network connectivity
  • Try disconnecting and reconnecting
  • Check if your target device is responding

Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Check network connection
    • Ensure Wi-Fi is enabled and connected
    • Test internet connectivity
  2. Verify device compatibility
    • Confirm your device supports the required protocols
    • Check that WebSocket communication is enabled
  3. Restart everything
    • Close and reopen the Magic Bullet app
    • Restart your mobile device
    • Power cycle your target device
  4. Reset app data
    • Clear the app's stored data
    • Re-enter your connection settings

System Requirements

  • iOS: iOS 12.0 or later
  • Network: Wi-Fi connection required
  • Device: Compatible devices with WebSocket support

Privacy & Data

Magic Bullet respects your privacy:

  • No personal data is collected or transmitted to external servers
  • All communication stays within your local network
  • Connection settings are stored only on your device
